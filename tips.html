Your Privacy Is Protected

GLNewsReports takes source protection extremely seriously. We follow the highest standards of journalistic ethics when handling tips.

  • Anonymous tips accepted
  • Source identity never disclosed
  • Documents handled securely
  • No identifying info published without consent
  • Tips reviewed by senior editorial staff

What Makes a Good Tip?

The most actionable tips include specific names, dates, locations, and documents. Even partial information can help us find the full story. If you have a sense something is wrong but aren't sure, tell us what you know. We'll take it from there.

What Happens Next?

Tips are reviewed by our editorial team within 48 hours. If your tip leads to an investigation, a reporter may reach out for a follow-up conversation. Not all tips result in published stories, but every submission is read and taken seriously.

Need extra security? For highly sensitive tips, consider using a secure email service or encrypted messaging. You can also mail documents to our editorial office. Contact us first to arrange secure delivery.
