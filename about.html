About GLNewsReports

Independent Journalism Rooted in Northeast Arkansas

GLNewsReports is the independent news venture of Grendel Levy, an Emmy Award-winning journalist and former News Director with more than 20 years of experience in broadcast and digital media. Based in Jonesboro, Arkansas, GLNewsReports is committed to honest, community-centered reporting for the people of Northeast Arkansas.

"Good journalism doesn't just report the news. It holds power accountable, gives voice to the voiceless, and fights for the communities that need it most."

About Grendel Levy

Grendel Levy is an Emmy Award-winning News Director originally from New Orleans, Louisiana. She began her journalism career at the ABC affiliate in New Orleans in the late 1990s and went on to build a broad skill set across nearly every role in a modern newsroom, including producer, executive producer, special projects producer and video editor, across multiple television markets.

Grendel has served as News Director in Boise, Idaho and Charleston, West Virginia, bringing more than a decade of management experience to her leadership roles. She is known for her cross-platform approach to news, delivering content to audiences on the platform, at the time, and in the format they want it.

As an Emmy Award-winning News Director, Grendel is not simply a manager but a leader who believes in teaching, mentoring and training journalists to do their best work. Her enthusiasm for the industry has long been a driving force for the teams she has led.

Grendel and her family are happy to be back in the South and now call Jonesboro home. When she is not in the newsroom, she enjoys spending time with her husband and children and loves cooking, especially Cajun style.
